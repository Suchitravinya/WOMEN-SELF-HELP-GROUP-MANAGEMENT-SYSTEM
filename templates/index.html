About Us

The Women Self-Help Group Management System is designed to empower women by fostering financial independence through **collaborative savings and loan management**.

We provide a **secure and transparent** way for women to manage their group funds, monitor savings, and facilitate easy loan distribution. Our mission is to **enhance financial literacy** and empower women to create a **stronger economic foundation** for themselves and their communities.
